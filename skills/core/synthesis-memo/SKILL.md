# Synthesis Memo

## Purpose

A synthesis is not a vague compromise.
It must preserve strengths from opposed positions and produce a higher-order path.

## Activate when

Use this skill after a real dialectical exchange has produced a plausible synthesis.

## Required sections

- thesis
- antithesis
- preserved strengths
- higher-order synthesis
- next step

## Output artifact

```markdown
## Synthesis Memo

### Thesis
- ...

### Antithesis
- ...

### Preserved Strengths
- From thesis: ...
- From antithesis: ...

### Higher-Order Synthesis
- ...

### Next Step
- ...
```

## Guardrails

- Do not call a weak average a synthesis.
- Do not erase the tension that made the synthesis valuable.
- Do not omit the operational next step.

## Completion condition

This skill is complete only when the synthesis is better than either side alone and can hand off to action.
